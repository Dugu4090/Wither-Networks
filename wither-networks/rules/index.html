Server Rules

Please read and follow these rules to ensure a fun and fair experience for everyone

General Rules

1. Respect All Players

Treat everyone with respect. Harassment, bullying, racism, sexism, or discrimination of any kind will not be tolerated. Keep chat friendly and appropriate for all ages.

Punishment: Mute → Temporary Ban → Permanent Ban

2. No Cheating or Hacking

Using hacked clients, x-ray mods, or any unfair advantage is strictly prohibited. This includes autoclickers, fly hacks, kill aura, and similar modifications.

Punishment: Permanent Ban (No Appeals)

3. No Exploiting

Do not exploit bugs, glitches, or server issues for personal gain. Report any bugs you find to staff immediately through our Discord or support tickets.

Punishment: Warning → Temporary Ban → Permanent Ban

4. No Spam or Advertising

Don't spam chat with repeated messages, caps, or unnecessary characters. Advertising other servers or services is not allowed.

Punishment: Warning → Mute → Temporary Ban

5. Respect Builds & Claims

Do not grief, steal from, or destroy other players' builds. Respect land claims and protected areas. Ask permission before building near others.

Punishment: Rollback + Temporary Ban → Permanent Ban

6. Listen to Staff

Always follow staff instructions. Disrespecting, arguing with, or ignoring staff members will result in punishment. If you disagree with a decision, appeal respectfully.

Punishment: Warning → Temporary Ban → Permanent Ban

Chat Rules

English Only in Main Chat

Please use English in the main chat so everyone can understand and staff can moderate effectively. You may use other languages in private messages.

No Personal Information

Never share personal information like your real name, address, phone number, or social media accounts in public chat. Protect your privacy.

No Inappropriate Content

Keep all content family-friendly. No NSFW content, excessive swearing, sexual content, or disturbing material allowed anywhere on the server.

No Scamming or Begging

Don't scam other players in trades or beg for items, ranks, or money. All trades should be fair and consensual.

Gameplay Rules

No AFK Farming

Excessive AFK farming or using AFK pools/machines to gain unfair advantages is not allowed. You may AFK, but not to exploit game mechanics.

Alt Account Limits

You may have up to 2 accounts on the server. Using alt accounts to bypass bans or exploit systems will result in all accounts being banned.

Appropriate Builds Only

Builds must be appropriate for all ages. No offensive, inappropriate, or disturbing structures. Staff may remove inappropriate builds without warning.

No Server Lag Machines

Do not create lag machines, excessive redstone contraptions, or anything that negatively impacts server performance.

PvP Rules

Respect PvP Zones

Only engage in PvP in designated PvP zones. In Survival, respect player preferences for PvP. No combat logging or unfair tactics.

No Spawn Camping

Do not repeatedly kill players at spawn points or safe zones. Give players a fair chance to defend themselves or escape.

Honor Agreements

If you agree to team with someone or make a deal, honor it. Breaking trusts and betrayals are part of the game but excessive toxic behavior may be punished.

No Combat Logging

Don't disconnect during combat to avoid death. Combat logging will result in your death and potential punishment if repeated.

Punishment Policy

How do I appeal a punishment?

You can appeal punishments through our Discord server in the #appeals channel. Provide your username, the reason for your ban, and why you think it should be lifted. Appeals are reviewed within 24-48 hours.

What if I see someone breaking the rules?

Use /report [player] [reason] in-game or create a ticket on our Discord. If you have evidence (screenshots/videos), include them in your report for faster processing.

Are there warnings before bans?

For minor offenses, yes. Most rules have a warning → temporary ban → permanent ban progression. However, serious offenses like hacking result in immediate permanent bans.

Can purchased ranks be refunded if I'm banned?

No, purchases are non-refundable. If you violate our rules and receive a ban, you will lose access to your rank. Make sure to follow all rules to keep your perks.

Important Notes

These rules are subject to change at any time. Staff have final say in all situations. Not knowing the rules is not an excuse for breaking them. By playing on WitherNetworks, you agree to follow all server rules and staff decisions.

If you're unsure about something, ask a staff member before doing it!
