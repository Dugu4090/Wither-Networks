Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get a response?

Discord messages typically receive responses within 1-2 hours. Support tickets are answered within 12-24 hours, and emails within 24-48 hours depending on complexity.

What's the best way to report a bug?

Create a support ticket or use the #bug-reports channel on Discord. Include detailed information about the bug, steps to reproduce it, and screenshots/videos if possible.

Can I get a refund on my purchase?

All purchases are final and non-refundable as stated in our terms of service. However, if you experience technical issues preventing you from receiving your rank, contact support immediately.

How do I apply for staff?

Staff applications are posted on our Discord when positions are available. Follow the application format and submit it in the designated channel. We review all applications carefully.
